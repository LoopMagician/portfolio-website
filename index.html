Projects

  • The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster)

This is my project partner's and I's rendition of The Legend of Zelda.

During development, I took part in early game mechanics, such as the camera and player movement, the Old Man Room, the 2D bow room, collectibles, doors, pushable objects, room set up, and the HUD. During the later stages of development, I came up with and implemented the flying Umbrella Mechanic as well as the Custom Level and everything that had to do with it. I thouroughly enjoyed developing the project and I am proud of the work that I did on it. Both my parter and I spent many hours working on it, and I can say that I feel good about the final product. I hope you enjoy it as much as we did making it.
